There are three types of QR codes: static, dynamic, and animated. Static QR codes are the most common type of QR code. Dynamic QR codes can store multiple pieces of information, such as a website URL, a phone number, and a text message. Animated QR codes are similar to static QR codes, but they can be used to display a short animation.
